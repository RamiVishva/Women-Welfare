Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana


This scheme was launched in 2018 by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare for covering up to 10 crore people belonging to poor and vulnerable families. Under this scheme, coverage of up to Rs. 5 lakhs per family for secondary and tertiary hospitalization is provided. With a broad spectrum of schemes launched by the Government of India, benefitting senior citizens belonging to various classes, planning for your health, and other related expenses has become easier.
